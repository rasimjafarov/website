Translation Services

Professional translation services across a wide range of industries and document types, ensuring linguistic accuracy, technical precision and cultural relevance.

  • Legal Documentation – contracts, agreements, powers of attorney, court decisions, corporate governance documents and regulatory submissions.
  • Military & Defence Translation - military and defence translation covering military specifications, technical manuals, operational procedures, logistics documentation, training materials and defence-related reports, with strict attention to accuracy and confidentiality.
  • Technical Documentation – manuals, specifications, procedures, engineering documentation and technical reports.
  • Medical & Pharmaceutical Documentation – medical records, patient information materials, pharmaceutical documentation, clinical reports and healthcare-related materials.
  • Oil & Gas & Energy Documentation – drilling and production operations documents, Operations Steering Committee Minutes of Meetings (MoMs), project reports, technical studies and field documentation.
  • HSE & Environmental Documentation – HSE manuals, ESIA reports, environmental assessments, sustainability and compliance documentation.
  • Business & Corporate Documentation – official correspondence, business communications, policies, procedures, feasibility studies and corporate reports.
  • Media & Publishing – news articles, press releases, website content, editorial materials and publication-ready texts.

Interpretation Services

Professional consecutive, liaison and conference interpreting services for meetings, negotiations, site visits, inspections, corporate events, international conferences, technical workshops and civil and military projects.

  • Conference Interpreting – international conferences, forums, summits and official events.
  • Oil & Gas Interpreting – offshore and onshore projects, drilling campaigns, site visits, HSE meetings and Operations Steering Committee sessions.
  • Military & Defence Interpreting – defence cooperation meetings, training activities and technical military discussions.
  • Government & Diplomatic Interpreting – official delegations, protocol meetings and international cooperation projects.

Editing & Proofreading

Professional editing and proofreading services delivered in collaboration with Charles Ainsworth, a native English-speaking professional linguist, with over 15 years of joint experience ensuring linguistic accuracy, stylistic consistency, terminology verification and publication-ready quality.

  • Native English Review – review and refinement of English-language texts by a native English-speaking professional linguist.
  • Proofreading – correction of grammar, spelling, punctuation, formatting and typographical errors.
  • Editing – improvement of clarity, style, tone, flow and readability while preserving the original meaning.
  • Terminology Verification – consistency checks for technical, legal, medical, business and academic terminology.
  • Quality Assurance – final linguistic review, formatting control and publication-ready delivery.

Language Consulting

Professional language consulting services covering localization strategy, multilingual communication, terminology management and cross-cultural communication support. We provide practical language solutions for businesses, organizations and international projects, with consultation and advisory support available by phone, email or online meetings on a 24/7 basis whenever required.

Literary Translation

Professional literary translation services for novels, memoirs, biographies, essays, poetry, drama, stage plays, screenplays and other creative works. Particular attention is paid to preserving the author's style, tone, cultural nuances, artistic expression and emotional impact while ensuring natural readability for the target audience.

  • Fiction & Novels – literary works, contemporary fiction and classic literature.
  • Poetry & Creative Writing – preservation of imagery, rhythm and artistic expression.
  • Drama, Stage Plays & Screenplays – performance-oriented texts, theatre scripts and dialogue-focused works.

About me

A Passion for Languages.
A Commitment to Excellence.

I am a professional translator, interpreter, editor and language consultant with more than 34 years of experience in the oil & gas industry, legal, technical, medical and literary fields.

Throughout my career, I have had the privilege to work with leading international organizations, including BP and the Azerbaijan International Operating Company (AIOC), and to serve as an official translator at major global events such as COP29 in Baku, Azerbaijan, and the World Urban Forum (WUF13).

In addition to my corporate and conference interpreting work, I am an accomplished literary translator and the author of translations of more than 28 stage plays by Azerbaijani, Russian, Israeli, Georgian and Spanish playwrights. My work has contributed to the promotion of intercultural dialogue and the introduction of international dramatic literature to new audiences.

I am currently working on two English-language book projects and continue to be actively involved in literary translation, editing and publishing activities.

As the founder and owner of CTS Translation and CTS Translation Plus, I provide professional language solutions to clients in Azerbaijan and internationally. I am also the publisher of CTS Baku Magazine, an electronic bulletin covering business, culture, international affairs and developments relevant to Azerbaijan and the wider region.

My mission is simple: to deliver accurate, culturally appropriate and impactful communication that connects people, bridges cultures and builds lasting trust.
